# Associate Software Engineer

*Location: Remote | Type: Full-time*
*CTC: 10-12 LPA*

About the company:

At Appbrew, our mission is to create the best shopping experience for global DTC brands. We are building an AI powered e-commerce app platform that enables brands to launch high-performance, feature-rich mobile apps that drive retention and revenue. Three years into our journey, we are proud to work with some of the largest DTC brands across India, the USA, Australia, and the UK.

Role:

We are seeking a Front-End Web Developer to join our team, primarily responsible for replicating designs using CSS on the web. This role offers an excellent opportunity for recent graduates with a keen eye for detail and a passion for turning designs into reality. As a Junior Front-End Web Developer, you will collaborate closely with our design and development teams to ensure seamless implementation of web designs.

Responsibilities:

- Translate design mock-ups into pixel-perfect, responsive web pages using HTML and CSS.
- Ensure consistency and accuracy in design implementation across various browsers and devices.
- Collaborate with designers and back-end developers to achieve the best possible user experience.
- Troubleshoot and debug CSS issues to ensure smooth functionality across different platforms.
- Stay updated on emerging trends and best practices in front-end development

Qualifications and Skills:

- Bachelor's degree in Engineering (BE), Technology (B.Tech), or Computer Applications (BCA).
- 0-2 years of experience in front-end web development.
- Proficiency in HTML and CSS with a strong understanding of CSS frameworks.
- Solid grasp of responsive design principles and mobile-first development.
- Attention to detail and ability to replicate designs accurately.
- Eagerness to learn and adapt to new technologies and methodologies.
